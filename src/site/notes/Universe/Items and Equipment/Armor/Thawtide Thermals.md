*Densely spun [[Universe/Locations/Dendria\|Dendrit]] weave to fend off the [[Universe/The World of Alca/Festivals/Thawtide\|Thawtide]] chills. 'Twas once tradition to feed [[Universe/Factions and Species/The Cardinals\|The Cardinals]] on each of the seven days leading to Thawtide. With leather gloves cupped 'round last autumn's seeds, families left their hearths and marched into the freeze. Upon gloved palms, landed tiny talons, feasting 'fore their hungry work at Thawtide's dawn. This year, hungrier than ever before.*
