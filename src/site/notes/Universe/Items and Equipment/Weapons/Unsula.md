*The binary blades of [[Universe/Factions and Species/Sinecure\|Sinecure]] mercenaries.*

*Each Unsulum dagger is cast in a measured arc. Some liken it to a broken orbit, others to a pinching claw. Together, the Unsula are precisely formed to strike soundlessly through [[Universe/Characters/Alca/Gods of Alca/Alca\|Alcan]] atmosphere.*
