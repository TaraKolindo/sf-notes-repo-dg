[[Universe/Characters/Alca/Dendria/The Pensives/Orlick\|Orlick]]: "The histories, gone! Ashes in the sky! (whispering) It is hopeless, little one. Hopeless! [[The Valadir Codices\|The Valadir Codices]]... [[Punja's Precious Poems\|Punja's Precious Poems]]... what soulless thing sets word to torch? By the mother's divine! An [[Universe/Factions and Species/Envoy\|Envoy]]! The [[Universe/Factions and Species/Ode\|Ode]] have burned the great [[Universe/Factions and Species/The Pensives\|Pensives]]' archive! Now words live only in smoke. (whispering) Perhaps you and I might do something about it? What do you say?"

Orlick: "I am Orlick the Bicameral. By folded spine and sacred bind, my quill is signed to you. Though inked in blood and cinder."

Orlick: "My sage's study, here, in fold of night! (sighs) So many genius works left half-written. There is no future for [[Universe/Events/The Dread Song\|a pastless people]], Envoy. Our minds and shelves emptied... (whispering) Yes. It seems time at last to finish my book... But what title is worthy of the [[Aurem Canus\|Aurem Canus]]' seal?!"
